Simple omelette recipe

an easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal,this calssic combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optinally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables, or meats

preparation time

  • Total: approximately 10 minutes
  • cooked: 5 minutes
  • preparation: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • peppre, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil

    • Instruction

    1. beat the eggs
    2. heat the pan
    3. cook the omelette
    4. add fillings
    5. fold and serve
    6. enjoy
